Bio

I am Gaddam Jagadish, I am from Telangana. I am a game developer, pursuing M.SC in Game Development and Game Design from AAFT Noida. I am open for Unity Development, Game Testing and level Designer roles.

I am a game developer proficient in C#, Unity, Python, and C++, with hands-on experience using Git, Unity Version Control, DOTween, Cinemachine and ProBuilder. I am passionate about gameplay programming and writing clean and well-structured code.

Projects

Teddy is a first-person horror shooter where you play as a special operations agent sent to investigate a mysterious anomaly in an abandoned house. Your mission is simple to uncover the source of the anomaly and make it out alive.

Desert Runner is a casual 2D platformer set in a vast desert world. Run, jump, and climb obstacles as you explore sandy landscapes and collect hidden treasures.

CSI Simulator - fruit mart mystery is a 3D isometric Investigative Mystery Game. You start at the murder scene located in the Fruit Mart, carefully examine the area for potential clues and identify the correct suspect and ensure justice is served.

Dune Rush is a fast-paced 3D infinite runner where you play as Loria, running across endless desert dunes to escape from a strange, unstoppable bear. Avoid obstacles, collect golden coins and stay ahead in the chase. How far can you go before the beast catches you?

Island Bird Hunter is a classic shooter inspired 2D Duck Hunt game. Aim and shoot at the birds flying across the screen to earn points, hit as many birds as possible before they escape. My first ever game made.

Wave Bird is a hyper casual game inspired by Flappy Bird. The goal is to keep flying as long as possible without crashing. Easy to play but hard to master. Try to get the highest score possible!

Pixel Invasion is a casual 2D Isometric puzzle Game.Your goal is to heal all the pixelated tiles and face the monster and catch it to restore the glory of vector art. With simple controls, it’s an easy-to-play yet addictive casual game.
